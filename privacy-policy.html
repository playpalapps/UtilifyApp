Privacy Policy

Utilify — Your everyday tech toolkit
Last updated: March 22, 2026

Summary: Utilify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All tool calculations happen locally on your device. We use Google AdMob to display ads, which may collect limited data as described below.

1. Information We Collect

Utilify itself does not collect any personal information. All data you enter into the app's tools (calculations, conversions, text) is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

2. Locally Stored Data

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Favorites: Tools you bookmark for quick access
  • Recent history: Tools you've recently used
  • Theme preference: Your light/dark mode choice

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time.

3. Advertising

Utilify uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (country/region level)
  • App usage data and interaction with ads
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)

This data is collected and processed by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. We do not have access to any personally identifiable information collected by Google AdMob.

4. Ad Personalization

Google may use the data collected through AdMob to show personalized ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking > disable tracking for Utilify
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

5. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For serving advertisements — Privacy Policy
  • Open Exchange Rates API: For live currency conversion rates (no personal data is sent; only a rate request is made)

6. Children's Privacy

Utilify is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Google AdMob is configured to comply with applicable child protection regulations.

7. Data Security

Since Utilify does not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk. All locally stored preferences are kept in standard device storage and are protected by your device's own security measures.

8. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete on our end. For data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for information on exercising your data rights.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
