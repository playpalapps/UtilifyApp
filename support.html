Utilify — Your everyday tech toolkit

Need help? We're here to assist you. Utilify is designed to be simple and intuitive, but if you run into any issues, check the common questions below or reach out to us directly.

Common Questions

How do I save a tool to favorites?

Long-press any tool card on the home screen, or tap the bookmark icon inside any tool.

How do I switch between light and dark mode?

Go to Settings and toggle the Dark Mode switch.

Are my calculations stored anywhere?

No. All calculations happen locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server. Your recent history only tracks which tools you opened, not the data you entered.

Why do I see ads?

Utilify is free to use. Ads help support ongoing development. We use Google AdMob and do not collect personal data ourselves.

How do I clear my history?

Go to the Recent tab and tap "Clear" in the top right corner.
